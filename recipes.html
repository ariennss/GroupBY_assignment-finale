MATCHA DORAYAKI


Ingredients


For the adzuki bean paste:

  • 150g adzuki beans
  • 100g beet sugar
  • a pinch of salt

For the batter:

  • 150g rice flour
  • 30g beet sugar
  • 2g baking soda
  • 8g matcha
  • 150g plant-based milk

Instructions


Put adzuki beans and water into a pan and bring to a boil. drain hot water then add water and simmer for 30 to 60 minutes until they softens. Add beet sugar and salt. Keep simmering until water is almost gone. Put all the ingredients for the batter and blend well. Let it rest in a fridge for 30 minuter. Cook the batter on a pan both sides. Sand the adzuki bean paste with the cakes. Enjoy!

ACAI ICE CREAM


Ingredients


  • 400g rice milk
  • 400g banana
  • 100g dates
  • 120g cashew nuts
  • 10g coconut Oil
  • 40g acai powder

Instructions


Blend all the ingredients with a high power blender. Put the mixture into an ice cream maker. It normally takes 15-20 minutes. Leave the mixture in a freezer for a couple of hours.

CINNAMON TWISTED ROLLS


Ingredients


  • 285g super fine rice flour
  • 30g beet sugar
  • 15g oil
  • 3g dry yeast
  • 3g salt
  • 330g warm water
  • 50g brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon powder

Instructions


Put all the ingredients for the dough in a stand mixer and blend well. Let it ferment untl it doubles in volume then put it in a fridge for a few hours. Dust the surface then roll out into rectangular shape. Spread brown sugar and cinnamon powder on top then shape the dough into twisted rolls. Let it ferment until it rises a bit. Bake at 180C/356F for 30 minutes. Enjoy!

MAIN DISHES

CINNAMON TWISTED ROLLS


Ingredients


  • 300g onion
  • 20g garlic
  • 20g ginger
  • 30g oil
  • 1 tps five spice powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 20g sesame paste
  • 20g dried tomato
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 500ml water
  • 100g lotus root
  • 100g spinach
  • 100g mushroom

Instructions


Fry onion on medium to high heat until brown. it takes 10 - 15 minutes. then add powder spice. in a blender, put sesame paste, dried tomato, brown sugar, salt and water together and blend well. Fry lotus root, mushroom and spinach in a pan. Add the onion paste and the mixture then simmer until it gets thicker. Enjoy!

PUMPKIN COCONUT MILK


Ingredients


  • 100g green lentils
  • 200g turnip
  • 200g pumpkin
  • 30g oil
  • green chili pepper
  • a sprig of fresh curry leaves
  • 1 tsp mustard seed
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 200ml water
  • 2 tsp salt

Instructions


Simmer green lentils and steam pumpkin and turnip. Ina pot, put oil and mustard seed. when it finishes popping, add green chili pepper and curry leaves. Add coconut milk, water, green lentils, pumpkin, turnip and salt. Enjoy!

PAD THAI


Ingredients


  • 100g rice noodles
  • 50g onion
  • a clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 200g firm tofu or deep fried tofu
  • 4 - 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 20g tamarind paste
  • water as needed
  • 50g bean sprouts
  • roasted peanuts
  • lime wedges

Instructions


Soak rice noodles for 10-30 minutes. Fry garlic and onion. Add tofu and seasonings. Put rice noodles into the pan and gently keep heating until the noodles get softens. Add water as needed. Add bean sprouts and garlic chives. Garnish with peanuts and lime wedges. Enjoy!

DRINKS

ICED SHISO LATTE


Ingredients


  • 350g red shiso leaves
  • 2000g water
  • 300g beet sugar
  • 300g apple cider vinegar
  • plant-based milk

Instructions


Boil red shiso leaves with 2 litter of water for 5 minutes. remove the leaves. Add beet sugar and apple cider vinegar. Mix with your favorite plant-based milk. Enjoy!

MATCHA BOBA LATTE


Ingredients


  • 50 black tapioca pearls
  • 15g sweetener
  • 150ml almond milk
  • 4g matcha
  • 60ml hot water

Instructions


Boil black tapioca pearls for about 60 minutes, then drain and rinse. Put tapioca pearls, almond milk and sweetener into a glass cup. Sift matcha and add hot water. Whisk matcha. Pour the matcha into the glass. Enjoy!

MINT CHOCOLATE CHIP FRAPPUCCINO


Ingredients


  • 150g ice
  • 100g oatmilk
  • 30g maple syrup
  • 3g mint leaves
  • 30g chocolate chips
  • 300g soy cream
  • 30g beet sugar

Instructions


Pour soy cream and beet sugar into a cream cylinder and charge it with N2O cream charger. make sure cream and cylinder are well chilled beforehand. Put ice, oat milk, mint leaves and maple syrup in a blender container and blend well. then add chocolate chips and blend a bit. Pour the frappuccino into a glass and top with whipped cream. Enjoy!
